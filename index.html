💡 How to Use This Calculator

Start with the classic 50/20/30 rule — 50% for essential needs, 20% for lifestyle wants, and 30% for savings & investments. Then drag the sliders to match your real-life situation. Any income left after your three categories is shown as Unallocated — flex money you can assign however you like. Total over 100%? Adjust until you're balanced!
